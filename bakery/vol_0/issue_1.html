issue 1 - cookies (malted hemp)

the "easiest" thing to bake...but the technique used and order of steps taken change the taste/texture

ingredients

amount measure ingredient inital state
1 cup flour
0.5 tsp baking soda
0.5 tsp salt
0.5 cup hemp seeds
0.25 cup white sugar
0.25 cup brown sugar
0.25 cup malted milk powder
0.5 cup butter melted
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla extract
0.5 cup chocolate chips

steps

  1. mix flour, baking soda, salt dry mix
  2. mix sugars, malted milk powder, butter, egg,vanilla wet mix
  3. fold dry mix and hemp seeds into wet mix
  4. add chocolate chips
  5. cool in fridge for 1-24 hrs
  6. scoop 1 tbsp per cookie on baking sheet
  7. bake @350F for 9-10 mins
  8. cool on baking sheet for 10 mins
  9. cool on rack for 10 mins

bakers notes

  • do not over mix
  • favorite cookie recipe so far
