chocolate lava cake

ingredients

amount measure ingredient
0.25 cup butter
2 oz chocolate
0.5 cup powdered sugar
1 egg
1 egg yolk
0.5 tsp vanilla extract
0.25 cup flour

steps

  1. melt butter, chocolate
  2. add sugar
  3. add egg, yolk
  4. add vanilla
  5. fold in flour
  6. split into 2 ramekins
  7. bake @425F for 12 mins
  8. coll in pan for 10 mins
  9. invert and serve

bakers notes

  • do not over mix
  • the cake is LAVA!

banana rum cake

ingredients

amount measure ingredient initial state
1.5 cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
0.5 tsp baking powder
0.5 tsp salt
0.5 tsp cinnamon
0.5 tsp cloves
0.25 tsp nutmeg
0.5 cup butter room temp
0.5 cup sugar
0.25 cup rum
3 banana mashed
2 tsp vanilla
2 eggs
0.25 cup sour cream
0.5 cup pecans

steps

  1. mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg dry mix
  2. mix butter, sugar untill fluffy wet mix
  3. add egg, vanilla, sour cream wet mix
  4. add banana, rum wet mix
  5. fold in wet mix and wet mix
  6. fold in pecans
  7. grease bundt pan
  8. bake @350F for 45 mins
  9. cool in pan for 15 mins
  10. invert and serve

bakers notes

  • the cake is RUMMY!
