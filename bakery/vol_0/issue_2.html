issue 2 - banana bread

the building block of home baking

ingredients

amount measure ingredient
3 large banana
2 cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
0.5 tsp salt
1.5 tsp cinnamon
0.5 cup white sugar
0.5 cup brown sugar
0.5 cup butter
2 large eggs
1.5 tsp vanilla extract
0.5 cup buttermilk

steps

  1. preheat over to 325F
  2. grease loaf pan with butter
  3. mash banana till creamy [banana mix]
  4. add lemon juice [banana mix]
  5. mix flour, baking soda, baking powderm cinnamon, salt [dry mix]
  6. cream butter with sugars [wet mix]
  7. add beaten eggs [wet mix]
  8. fold in [banana mix] with [wet mix]
  9. combine [dry mix] and [wet mix] (careful not to over mix)
  10. pour into loaf pan
  11. bake for 1 hr + time for stick to come out clean from center
  12. cool in pan for 5 mins
  13. cool on rack for 1 hr
